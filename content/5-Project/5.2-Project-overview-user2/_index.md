# Exam Results Management & Classroom Space

This section introduces the features for tracking examination history and interacting within individual classrooms on the **Aura Academic** platform.

---

### 1. Exam Results & Learning History

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.1: Exam Results Page Interface](image.png)

**Figure 5.1. Exam Results Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Overview Statistics:** Instantly displays Total Exams Taken, Average Score, Pass Rate, and Highest Score achieved.
- **Filtering & Quick Search:** Versatile filters by keyword (Exam Code, Title), Status (Passed / Failed), and Source, making it easy to review past assessments.
- **Detailed Exam Breakdown:** Each result entry can be expanded to inspect completion time, exact score, and qualitative grading tags.

**In-Depth Classroom Activities:**
- **Assigned Exams & Exercises:** Centralized list of class-specific tests, clearly indicating submission deadlines and completion status.
- **Transparent Gradebook:** Detailed personal grades for every assessment, allowing students to self-monitor progress across different subjects.
- **Discussion Forum (Q&A):** Interactive space for students to ask questions, share insights, and communicate directly with teachers and peers.
