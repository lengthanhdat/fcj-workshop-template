# Exam Administration, Question Editor, Media & Audit Logs

This section provides an in-depth look at large-scale exam management, advanced question authoring tools, media repositories, and security monitoring (Audit Logs) for Administrators on **Aura Academic**.

---

### 1. Exam Administration & Comprehensive Reporting

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.1: Exam Administration Page Interface](image.png)

**Figure 5.1. Exam Administration Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Centralized Exam Setup:** Create, configure opening/closing schedules, set secure exam room codes, and assign question papers for school-wide or system-wide examinations.
- **Monitoring & Analytics:** Track active student participation in real time and review comprehensive statistical breakdowns upon exam completion.

---

### 2. Advanced Question Authoring Editor

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.2: Question Editor Page Interface](image-1.png)

**Figure 5.2. Question Editor Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Rich-Text Authoring:** Intuitive editor supporting single/multiple-choice questions, essay prompts, mathematical formulas (LaTeX), and illustrative images.
- **Automated Grading & Explanations:** Input exact answer keys and detailed explanations to streamline automated grading and immediate student feedback.

---

### 3. Global Question Bank Administration

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.3: Question Bank Page Interface](image-2.png)

**Figure 5.3. Question Bank Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Structured Categorization:** Organize exam questions by subject, grade level, and difficulty taxonomy (Recognition, Understanding, Application, Advanced).
- **Batch Import/Export:** Bulk-import questions from standardized Word/Excel templates to accelerate question repository expansion.

---

### 4. Study Materials & Curriculum Management

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.4: Document Management Page Interface](image-3.png)

**Figure 5.4. Document Management Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Resource Publishing:** Upload and distribute official curriculum documents, e-books, and reference guides across the platform.
- **Access Control:** Configure visibility settings to make documents public across the school or restricted to specific grade levels/departments.

### 5. Content & Media Library Management

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.5: Content & Media Page Interface](image-4.png)

**Figure 5.5. Content & Media Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**
- **Centralized File Repository:** Manage all lecture videos, images, and attachments utilized across courses and examinations.

### 6. System Audit Logs

<div align="center">

![Figure 5.6: Audit Logs Page Interface](image-5.png)

**Figure 5.6. Audit Logs Page Interface**

</div>

**Key Features:**

### 7. User Session Management

<div align="center">
